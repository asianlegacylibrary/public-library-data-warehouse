SPRI YANG GLA SGANG SPRA TSIL BA LE KA, ,PRI KA SHING TSA RGYA
SPOS SPANG SPOS TZAN DAN SEN MO THANG CHU KUN TUR RNAMS, ,SBYOR
BA SBOS RGYAL GLA RTZI GA PUR DAG GIS GSO ZHING BDUG PA MCHOG GIS
BDUG ,ME TOG ZHIM POS BSGO BGYIS DANG DRI SBYAR

MCHOG TU 'DI 'GYUR LUS MCHOG MA, ,SHING TSA AA KAR BA TRA PRI KA
TU RUS SPANG SPOS RGYA SPOS RDO DREGS DANG, ,KUN DU GAN DHA BA
LIG THANG CHU PRI YANG SEN MO CAN DAN GLA SGANG RNAMS, ,GO RIMS
BZHIN DU CHA RE BSKYED CING DRI'I SBYOR BA BRGYAD PA PHRAG GNYIS
DANG, ,BZHI BA'I SBYOR BA'ANG NYI SHU LAS NI GNYIS

KYIS NYUNG BAR GYIS SHIG 'DZANGS MA MO, ,AA KAR KUN TUR TU RUS
GAN DHA THANG CHU PRI YANG TZAN DAN GNAS RNAMS SU, ,SUG SMEL PA
MU RU RTA BYA KU RE'U MIG TZOR GZHAG BYA ZHING, ,SHING TSA'I GNAS
SU GRAN TI BZHAG CING DE YI CHA LAS GSUM DU BSGYUR BYAS TE, ,DE
DAG SBYOR BA RE LTAR BYAS PAS KHRUS

KYI CHO GA'I SBYOR BA'I RNAM PA YIN, ,SHA TA MA RTAN MUR PI DRI
LI NI DE BA N'A GE SAR, ,DRAN TSE'I GNAS SU SENG PHROM HA MAN
GSUM BZHAG DRI PHYI'I SBYOR BA YIN, ,SHING TSA TA MAL PRI YANG
TZAN DAN RU RTA SENG PHROM DANG NI SHU DAG DANG, ,STHE'U NA CHA
DRUG LDAN PA'I RIM PA BA LANG PHRU GU LTA BUR SREG PA YIN,

BA STE, ,RGYA SKYEGS DU RU KA DANG SPRA TSIL SBYAR BA NGAG NI
